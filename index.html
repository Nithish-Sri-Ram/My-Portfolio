Smart Meet AI

I developed the "Multilingual AI Meeting Minutes and Summarization Generator" using Flutter, which offers a meetings transcript accurately and serves a big userbase. I integrated APIs into the application to automate the creation of meeting minutes, enhancing the process with advanced speaker identification and improved transcription accuracy. This project efficiently streamlines meeting documentation, providing users with accurate, real-time summaries in multiple languages.

Voice Bridge

I developed a mobile application designed to support deaf and mute individuals, enabling real-time call transcription with seamless communication during live conversations. Built using Python, FastAPI, Asterisk, FreePBX, and Flutter, the app focuses on Indian languages by training a custom model to accurately support multiple regional languages. Integrated with VoIP systems, the app delivers live call transcription with high accuracy, enhancing communication efficiency. It empowers differently-abled individuals by offering real-time voice-to-text and text-to-voice functionalities, creating an accessible and inclusive solution for effective communication.
